The Department of Economic Sciences at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur will organize its Research Scholars' Day 2026. We welcome you to attend the conference, and present your research.

Call For Papers

We invite submissions of high-quality research papers in all areas of economics and development. Both theoretical and empirical papers are welcome. Please see the submission guidelines for more details.

  • Notification of Acceptance: January 5, 2026
  • Registration Deadline: January 15, 2026
  • Conference Dates: February 13 - 14, 2026

Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit their research papers for presentation at the conference. All submissions must be original and unpublished work. Papers should be submitted in PDF format via the online submission portal.

Paper Format

  • Full paper without author details (consisting of title, abstract within 250 words, keywords, main text, figures, tables, references, etc.).
  • Title page (title, author names and affiliations, corresponding author with email ID and contact number).
  • Use Times New Roman 12 font size, 1.5 space, and 1-inch (2.5 cm) margin all around, number all pages.
  • The maximum length of the paper should not exceed 9000 words (including all tables, appendices and references).
